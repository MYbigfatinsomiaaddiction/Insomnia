Treatments for Insomnia

Basic tips:

  • Stick to a sleep schedule. Keep your bedtime and wake time consistent from day to day, including on weekends.
  • Avoid large meals and beverages before bed.
  • Chamomile is another commonly used herb for the treatment of insomnia. You should not take it if you're sensitive to ragweed or chrysanthemums or other members of the compositae family.
  • Ashwagandha is a greattreatment for insomnia. A study suggests 300 milligrams two times a day can help you sleep better.
  • Exercise can help you sleep better, Try to aim for a 20-30 minute routine daily which can help you give you more energy when you're awake.
  • Sleeping pills requested/trusted by your doctor.
  • Behavior therapy.
